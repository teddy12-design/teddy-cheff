# CHEFTEDDY - Ethiopian Food Delivery Website

A beautiful food delivery website for authentic Ethiopian cuisine.

## Features

- **Authentication System**: Login page with authentication flow
- **Menu Page**: Display of authentic Ethiopian dishes with real images
- **Shopping Cart**: Add items to cart with sidebar display
- **Payment System**: Ethiopian payment methods including:
  - Telebirr (Mobile Money)
  - Commercial Bank of Ethiopia (CBE)
  - Awash Bank
  - Bank of Abyssinia
  - Dashen Bank
  - NIB Bank
  - Cash on Delivery
- **Order Confirmation**: Review and confirm orders with payment processing

## Pages

1. **login.html** - Login page (first page users see)
2. **index.html** - Menu page (requires login)
3. **confirm.html** - Order confirmation and payment (requires login)

## Usage

1. Open `login.html` in your browser
2. Enter any email and password to login
3. Browse the menu and add items to cart
4. Click "Proceed to Checkout" to go to payment page
5. Select payment method and complete order

## Design

- White background

- Cart sidebar on the right

## Ethiopian Dishes

All dishes are authentic Ethiopian foods with Amharic names:
- Doro Wat (ዶሮ ወጥ)
- Tibs (ጥብስ)
- Kitfo (ክትፎ)
- Shiro (ሽሮ)
- Beyaynetu (በያይነቱ)
- Injera (እንጀራ)
- Misir Wat (ምስር ወጥ)
- Gomen (ጎመን)
- Ful (ፉል)

All prices are in BIRR (Ethiopian currency).
